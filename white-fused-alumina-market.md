<p><p>White Fused Alumina (WFA) is segmented into two primary market types: Refractory & Ceramic Grade WFA and Abrasive Grade WFA. Refractory & Ceramic Grade WFA is utilized in high-temperature applications due to its excellent thermal stability and low impurities, making it ideal for ceramics, glass, and refractory materials. In contrast, Abrasive Grade WFA is favored in grinding and polishing products because of its hardness and sharp edges, commonly found in abrasives, sandpaper, and industrial cleaners. Both types cater to distinct industry needs.</p></p>
