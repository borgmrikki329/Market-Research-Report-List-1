<p><p>Fluid shortening is a type of fat used primarily in the food industry to enhance texture, moisture, and flavor in baked goods and confections. Unlike solid shortenings, fluid shortenings are liquid or semi-liquid, making them easy to blend with other ingredients. Overall, the fluid shortening market is positioned for robust growth as it adapts to evolving consumer preferences and industry innovations.</p></p>
